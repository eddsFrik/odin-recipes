Hot Chocolate

Description

This decadent crockpot hot chocolate is easy to make with milk, condensed milk, cream, chocolate chips, and vanilla in the slow cooker.

Ingredients

Here's what you'll need to make the best slow cooker hot chocolate of your life:

  • 6 cups milk: This easy slow cooker hot chocolate starts with regular milk (whole or reduced-fat will work best). If you use an alternative milk, opt for something creamy like oat milk.
  • 2 cups chocolate chips: Semisweet chocolate chips create a perfectly balanced flavor. You could also use milk or dark chocolate chips.
  • 1 ½ heavy whipping cream: Heavy whipping cream is the key to a creamy, decadent, rich slow cooker hot chocolate.
  • 1 (14 ounce) sweetened condensed milk: Like so many decadent recipes, this hot chocolate calls for a can of sweetened condensed milk.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla: A teaspoon of vanilla extract lends complexity and takes the overall flavor up a notch.

Steps

  1. Combine milk, chocolate chips, cream, condensed milk, and vanilla in a slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on Low, stirring occasionally, until chocolate chips are melted and mixture is smooth, about 2 hours.
